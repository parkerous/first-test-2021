# Binsu — Self-Study Notes

**BINSU: Volleyball** — a fast-paced Roblox volleyball game by BINSU Project,
inspired by the *Haikyuu!!* anime. Tagline seen online: "volleyball but with risk
of concussion." Separated into its own file (per request); BVL & CVR notes live in
[`bvl-cvr-notes.md`](./bvl-cvr-notes.md).

> Note: compiled from written online guides, not from watching gameplay directly.
> Binsu uses a **different, simpler control scheme** than BVL/CVR — don't mix them
> up. Confirm against the in-game tutorial, controls change between updates.

---

## Controls

Binsu does **not** use the BVL/CVR `1`-toggle layout. It's contextual — the same
input does different things on the ground vs in midair.

| Action | PC | Console |
|---|---|---|
| Bump (ground) / Spike (midair) | Left Click | `RT` |
| Set (ground) / Block (midair) | `Q` | `LT` |
| Dive | `Ctrl` | `X` |
| Serve | Click (when it's your serve) | `RT` |

**Key idea:** *one button, two jobs.*
- The **Click / `RT`** button = **bump** when you're on the ground, **spike** when
  you're in the air.
- The **`Q` / `LT`** button = **set** when on the ground, **block** when in the air.

---

## The four core skills in Binsu

**Serve**
1. When it's your turn, click (or `RT`) to start the serve.
2. Click **again** to choose power.
3. Then you can **jump and spike** the serve (tilt to aim direction), **or** let
   the ball drop and **bump serve** it in.
4. Curve serves are possible — aim/tilt to bend the ball's path.

**Spike**
- Jump, then Click / `RT` at the top of the jump.
- **Tilt to change the ball's direction** mid-spike — Binsu leans into aiming,
  fitting its fast Haikyuu-style pace.
- Meet the ball at peak height for max power.

**Set**
- On the ground, press `Q` / `LT` to set the ball up for a teammate's spike.
- A clean, high set is what makes the spike possible — this is the setup half of
  the bump-set-spike loop.

**Bump / Receive**
- On the ground, Click / `RT` to bump the incoming ball up.
- Get under it and control it toward your setter rather than swatting it back.

**Block**
- In midair at the net, press `Q` / `LT` to block.
- Time your jump to the opponent's spike contact point.

**Dive** (`Ctrl` / `X`)
- Emergency reach for balls out of normal range — for digs/saves.

---

## Feel & tips
- **Fast + physical** — the "risk of concussion" tagline reflects its arcadey,
  high-speed, body-contact feel vs the more sim-like BVL/CVR.
- **Learn the ground-vs-air rule first.** Most beginner mistakes in Binsu come
  from expecting a spike but doing a bump (or vice-versa) because you weren't in
  the air. Internalize: on the floor = bump/set, in the air = spike/block.
- **Aim your spikes** with tilt — direction control is a bigger deal here than raw
  power.
- Practice the serve-choose-power-then-spike-or-drop flow until it's automatic.
